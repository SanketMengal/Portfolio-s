What is DevOps and DevOps lifecycle?

By: Sanket Mengal 456 264

DevOps (short for Development and Operations) is a set of practices, principles, and tools that aims to automate and integrate the work of software development (Dev) and IT operations (Ops) as a means to shorten the systems development lifecycle and provide continuous delivery of high-quality software. DevOps emphasizes collaboration, communication, and automation between developers and IT operations teams to improve the efficiency, reliability, and speed of software deployment.
